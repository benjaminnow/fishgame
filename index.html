Sustainable Fishing Game

Instructions:

  1. Goal: Survive 30 seconds and you WIN
  2. If human satisfaction or fish population bars reach 0, GAME OVER
  3. Time survived after 30 seconds is BONUS
  4. Click and drag your mouse cursor to catch fish

Hints:

  • The fish regenerate the quickest when the fish population is at 50%
  • Humans lose satisfaction at an exponential rate with respect to how satisfied they are currently
    • FASTEST loss when satisfaction high
    • SLOWEST loss when satisfaction low
